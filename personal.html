Travis Scott's Personal Life

Travis Scott, born Jacques Webster II, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter who has been making waves in the music industry. He was born on April 30, 1992, in Houston, Texas. Scott's rise to fame has been accompanied by his colorful personal life, which has drawn public interest.

In addition to his musical success, Travis Scott's relationship with media personality Kylie Jenner has been highly publicized. The couple, who have a daughter named Stormi, have become one of Hollywood's most famous families. Scott and Jenner have been in the spotlight, balancing their personal lives with their high-profile careers.

Aside from his relationship with Kylie Jenner, Travis has also been involved in several philanthropic efforts, including his Astroworld Festival, which combines his love for music and his desire to give back to the community. His efforts extend beyond music, as he continues to use his platform to influence and inspire others.
